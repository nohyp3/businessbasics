Accounting

What is accounting?

Accounting is the analyzing and recording of numbers in a company

Terms to know:

  • Asset - Something of value examples: cash, land, buildings, office supplies, equipment
  • Balance Sheet - A financial statement that reports a company's assets, liabilities and shareholder equity at a speciifc point in time. They can also provide the long term financial position of a company.
  • Income Sheet - One of the financial statements of a company that shows revenue and expenses within a period of time.
  • Accounts Recievable - Someone owing for a service or good
  • Balance - asset = liability + owner's equity
  • Liability - debts of business or person listed in order of paying off
  • Owner's equity - Claim against a company's assets
  • Personal equity - a person's net worth
